About this project

My Students

My teaching philosophy is to teach a love of life-long learning. I hope to instill that in every child that walks through my door.

We live in a mostly rural area. At least 70% of our students are on free or reduced lunch. About 30% of our students are high transiency students. I might just have a student one or two weeks before he or she leaves for another school. The students are wonderful kids! They are very loving and truly enjoy each and every day at school.

My Project

My students need 20 subscriptions to Scholastic News magazines in order to read real life articles that appeal to them. They also need three sets of Classroom Classics books on CD to use at their listening center. Our students are expected to sit at their desks for most of the day. The one hour of freedom that they have during centers needs to be interesting and engaging.

The listening center will give them a chance to listen to someone else read a story while they follow along in the book. There will be follow up questions for each book for them to think about and answer. Another center will be using the Scholastic Reader magazine. These magazines have up-to-date articles and colorful pictures of items that interest the seven- and eight-year old students.

Thank you!
